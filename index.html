AUSTIN MAKACHOLA BIOGRAPHY

A few details about him

If you have been reading books related to art and history, you have probably come across the name "Austin Makachola" — a Kenyan-born mathematician and technologist who is currently making waves through hisinnovations, particularly in AI and software development. He has declared that the time for African innovation has come, and that African technology should stand alongside innovations from other regions of the world. He admires Japan, China, and Singapore which have demonstrated that technological backwardness can be confronted and overcome despite the odds. But who is he?

️
He was born Austin Odhiambo Makachola on 2nd July 1997 in Kenya, in the small sugarcane-growing town of Awendo, Migori County, to Mr. Dalmas Peter Makachola (a fine arts lecturer) and Hana Awuor (a businesswoman). He was the last born among all his half-siblings.

He now lives in Westlands in Kenya's capital, Nairobi. He is unmarried although rumors has it that he's dating beauty model; Linet Wesonga, an acquaintance of his from an early age.
He attended an elite high-school; St. Joseph’sSchool, Rapogi Boys. (established in 1962 by the catholic church). There he was active both in academics and student politics. He was throughout he stay overseeing no less than 300 students. In 2015, he vied for the school presidency, but was prevented from appearing kn the ballot, because the school administration felt his populist ideologies would compromise his duties as president. He retained his department chairmanship and was heavily influential in the council that was formed.

In 2017, having easily attained University entry point, he missed a chance to study law at the University of Nairobi, but was instead offered admission to study Mathematics and Computer Programming at the well endowed Jomo Kenyatta University.

He would graduate in 2023 (variance of one year, as universities closed nationwide in 2019, due to coronarirus pandemic.

A year later, he recieved invitation to pursue a Master’s degree in Energy at the Switzerland based, Cumbria University
Prior to graduating from Jomo Kenyatta University, Austin Makachola first worked as a banker, a position he held for one year. After receiving his Bachelor's degree in Statistics in 2023, he was employed as a network technician at an internet services company.

However, he later resigned due to what he felt were unfair working conditions. He criticized the medium-sized company for lacking defined job groups—especially those based on education—and for the uneven access to company resources such as vehicles.

Following his break from Mawingu, he was invited to lead a team of executives, managing the Ngong road branch of the Botswana based institution, Letshego. He currently works with the company. He has further instrumenta in esbtablishing the software development firm NEXAAfrica
